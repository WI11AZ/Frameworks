The CyberAlign Project

includes THREE distinct but complementary applications

Welcome to:

CyberRoles - ATLAS

(bêta v0.1.2)

CyberRoles – ATLAS is designed to support organizations in systematically mapping their specific requirements—covering knowledge, technical skills, and behavioral competencies—associated with each position within the organizational structure.

This mapping process facilitates alignment with established canonical workforce reference frameworks to ensure both role adequacy and effective task execution.
